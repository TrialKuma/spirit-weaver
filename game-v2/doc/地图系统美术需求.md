## 1. 地图背景 x3（每幕各一张）

**文件命名**：`bg_map_act1.png` / `bg_map_act2.png` / `bg_map_act3.png`
**尺寸**：1920x1080px

**通用要求**：
- 竖向卷轴地图的底图，需要在上面叠加节点和连线，所以画面整体偏暗、朦胧
- 不能有过于抢眼的焦点物体，要让节点 UI 成为视觉主体
- 边缘自然过渡到黑色（方便与 UI 融合）

**各幕差异化**：

### bg_map_act1.png -- 第 1 幕「月下荒野」
- **主色调**：冷蓝灰 + 银白月光
- **空间结构**：开阔的荒野平原，远处地平线可见破败城堡剪影
- 地面是荒草与碎石，零星枯树，没有明确道路
- 天空占画面上半，巨大苍白月亮提供冷光
- 雾气从地面升起，前景暗、远景略亮
- 整体要「空旷、荒凉、起点」的感觉

**Prompt**:
> Dark fantasy game background, desolate moonlit wasteland at night, vast open barren plains with dead grass and scattered rocks, a few twisted leafless trees silhouetted against the sky, ruined castle outline on distant horizon, massive pale moon in upper portion casting cold silver-blue light, mist rising from ground, foreground dark fading to black at edges, no road or path visible, cold blue-grey and silver color palette, atmospheric and lonely, painterly digital art style, 1920x1080, dark enough to serve as UI backdrop with game nodes overlaid on top

### bg_map_act2.png -- 第 2 幕「深渊神殿」
- **主色调**：暖琥珀金 + 深棕黑
- **空间结构**：巨大地下神殿内部的纵深俯瞰，从高处向下看
- 巨型石柱排列，地面有发光的金色魔法阵
- 熔岩或琥珀色光源从裂缝中透出，烛火点点
- 墙壁有巨大浮雕面孔，半掩在阴影中
- 整体要「宏伟、压迫、古老神秘」的感觉

**Prompt**:
> Dark fantasy game background, interior of a massive underground temple viewed from above, enormous stone pillars arranged in rows, glowing golden magical circles and runes carved into the floor, warm amber light seeping through cracks in the ground, scattered candle lights, colossal carved stone faces on walls half-hidden in shadow, deep brown-black and warm amber-gold color palette, sense of ancient grandeur and oppression, edges fade to pure black, painterly digital art style, 1920x1080, dark enough to serve as UI backdrop with game nodes overlaid on top

### bg_map_act3.png -- 第 3 幕「虚空星海」
- **主色调**：深紫 + 品红/洋红能量色
- **空间结构**：完全脱离地面，漂浮在虚空中，没有地平线
- 碎裂的大陆碎片悬浮在星空中，大小不一
- 背景是深紫色星云/能量漩涡，有粉红-洋红色的能量丝线
- 远处有巨大的裂缝/传送门轮廓，散发不祥光芒
- 整体要「超自然、末日、终焉」的感觉，和前两幕形成强烈反差

**Prompt**:
> Dark fantasy game background, floating in cosmic void with no ground or horizon, shattered continent fragments and broken stone platforms suspended in space at various sizes, deep purple nebula and energy vortex in background, pink-magenta energy tendrils weaving between floating debris, distant massive rift portal glowing ominously, scattered star-like particles, deep purple and magenta color palette contrasting with black void, surreal otherworldly apocalyptic atmosphere, edges fade to pure black, painterly digital art style, 1920x1080, dark enough to serve as UI backdrop with game nodes overlaid on top
